Questions

Good to know

What ages is Dorlio for?

Dorlio is designed for children roughly aged 2–10. You set each child’s age, and stories are written with age-appropriate vocabulary and length.

How long does a story take to make?

Under 60 seconds. The story text appears almost instantly, and the illustrations fade in chapter by chapter as you start reading.

Is it safe for my child?

Yes. Every input and generated story is screened by automated content safety filtering, and you can always review a story before reading it aloud. There are no ads and no tracking.

Do I have to pay?

No. The free plan includes 3 fully-illustrated stories every month. Premium ($4.99/month) unlocks unlimited stories, your saved library, PDF export, and unlimited child profiles.

What information do you collect about my child?

Only what you enter to personalize a story — a first name, an age, and a chosen avatar emoji. We don’t collect a child’s contact information, location, photos, or anything else. Read the full Privacy Policy.

Which devices does it run on?

Dorlio is a mobile app for iPhone, iPad, and Android. The iPad “read together” layout is a favorite for bedtime.
